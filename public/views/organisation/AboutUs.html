How it works

We've made it easy to give what you want, to who you want, when you want.

Put your gift
out there

It’s as simple as clicking ‘Add a gift’ and filling out the details of the skills, help or thing you’d like to give to someone in your community. Don’t forget a photo of the item or one that represents the skill or help you’re gifting.

Choose who
receives it

Whether it’s help for one or a skills session for many, it’s up to you who gets your gift! Select from the Kindo users who have requested your gift, using their heart score and reviews as a guide to their reputation as a giver and receiver.

Connect with them

Once you’ve chosen who to give your gift to, you’ll make arrangements with them to meet up and give them their gift. You’ll send a message - perhaps suggesting a date, time and location - to your chosen receiver to set the gifting in motion.

Rate your experience

After you’ve given (or received!) a gift, you’ll rate how the experience made you feel. Hopefully, both giver and receiver will be thrilled! The ratings you receive on Kindo affect your heart score - Kindo’s reputation system.
