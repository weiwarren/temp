Why giftuu?

Giftuu originated from the idea of gift economies - where social status is determined not by what you control but by what you give away.

Giftuu was created by a group of people who believe:

  • the world can be a better place - more connected, healthier an happier
  • everyone has something to give
  • giving creates happiness

We promise...

  • To always do the right thing by our users
  • To never let business interfere with your ability to connect with people in your community
  • To make it easy for you to connect with and give to people in your community
  • To not be political
  • To never sell your data
  • To not be evil. EVER.
